About Us

African Proficient Group is a holding company based in South Africa, with key interest in information Technology, Distribution, Management Consulting, and Media.

The underlying characteristics of the team at APG and its subsidiaries is highly driven by continuous drive of; innovative skills development, growth, global strategic thinking, credibility, integrity, good governance, humanity and the skill of ensuring a high level of presence in the industries or areas the group is vested in. APG takes great pride in its team being of the calibre of global leaders with an emerging class of professionalism that is adept at operating in international and multicultural contexts.

Corporate Social Investment

The great and legendary Nelson Mandela once said “Education is the most powerful weapon you can use to change the world”. At African Proficient Group we strongly believe in uplifting communities through educational programs. We continuously walk this talk through our upliftment projects. Education is priority since we believe if there is any change which can be effected, such will be driven by education.

  • Valdezia 100

    Valdezia100 is a tertiary education fund which assists students from the impoverished village of Valdezia in Limpopo, who then intend to give back to the community where they come from. The program helps in funding University tuition, accommodation costs and living allowance. African Proficient Group is a sponsor and partner of this organization which has seen high performing students accessing university studies. The organization was established in 2013, seeing its first student register at the University of Limpopo Medical school in 2014. It now boasts a number of students both in medical school, commerce faculties and law faculties.

  • Leamogetswe Safety Home

    Leamogetswe Safety Home is a registered NPO / Child & Youth Care Centre situated in Atteridgeville (Saulsville) west of Pretoria. African Proficient Group is a proud supporter and donor, and has adopted the home in the form of caring for the improvised teenage girls. We believe in affecting positive change through afterschool educational activities, such as young successful motivational speakers who discuss various educational topics with the girls, educational games, pampering sessions and goodie bags to raise their confidence, and inspire the girls to succeed and rise above their circumstances.

  • The Graduate School of Business

    The Graduate School of Business' commitment is to research and development, mainly in the area of innovation. African Proficient Group has partnered with the UCT Graduate School of Business, Centre for Emerging Markets, as we strongly believe that current problems will not be solved with current available solutions. The innovation supported by the disciplined research has proven timeously to be what it is needed to solve complex matters.
